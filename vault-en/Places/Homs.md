# Homs (حمص)

Central city of the Ka'b-Nawf-Dhū'l-Kilā' network. Umayyad power base after [[Places/Damascus|Damascus]].

## Significance

Homs is the city where **Himyarite** tribes from Yemen settled after the conquests. Ka'b's and Dhū'l-Kilā''s clan forms a **tribal power nucleus** integrated into the Umayyad apparatus: Ka'b for religious legitimation, Dhū'l-Kilā' for military force.
