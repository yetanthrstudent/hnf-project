# Hammām ibn Munabbih

Brother of [[Wahb ibn Munabbih]]. Principal transmitter of [[Abū Hurayra]] — author of the famous **Ṣaḥīfa of Hammām** (one of the oldest hadith collections).

## Role in the network

The family link Hammām (brother of Wahb) → transmits from Abū Hurayra creates a **structural connection** between the family of Wahb/Ka'b and the most prolific transmitter of prophetic hadiths.
