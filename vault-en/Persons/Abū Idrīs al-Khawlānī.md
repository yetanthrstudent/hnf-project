# Abū Idrīs al-Khawlānī

**ʿĀʾidh Allāh ibn ʿAbd Allāh**, qāḍī of Damascus under **ʿAbd al-Malik ibn Marwān** (Dimashq [p. 11743](https://shamela.ws/book/71/11743)). Born the year of Ḥunayn (8 H), during the Prophet's lifetime. Second ṭabaqa of the tābiʿūn of al-Shām (Ibn Samīʿ, Dimashq [p. 11757](https://shamela.ws/book/71/11757)).

## The hadith of carnivores and al-Zuhrī's admission

A major case of transmission of a prohibition **unknown in the Ḥijāz**, propagated from al-Shām. In Ṣaḥīḥ Muslim (no. 1932, [p. 5956](https://shamela.ws/book/711/5956)):

> Ḥarmala ibn Yaḥyā ← Ibn Wahb ← Yūnus ← **Ibn Shihāb [al-Zuhrī]** ← **Abū Idrīs al-Khawlānī** ← Abū Thaʿlaba al-Khushanī:
>
> « نَهَى رَسُولُ اللهِ ﷺ عَنْ أَكْلِ كُلِّ ذِي نَابٍ مِنَ السِّبَاعِ »
>
> « The Messenger of God prohibited eating any fanged animal among the carnivores. »

Then **al-Zuhrī** adds:

> « وَلَمْ أَسْمَعْ ذَلِكَ مِنْ عُلَمَائِنَا بِالْحِجَازِ حَتَّى حَدَّثَنِي أَبُو إِدْرِيسَ، وَكَانَ مِنْ فُقَهَاءِ أَهْلِ الشَّامِ »
>
> « **I never heard this from our scholars in the Ḥijāz**, until Abū Idrīs told me — and he was among the jurists of al-Shām. »

### Analysis

This admission by al-Zuhrī is critical: the greatest scholar of the Ḥijāz acknowledges that a **major dietary prohibition** was unknown to him until a **Umayyad judge from al-Shām** transmitted it to him. This same prohibition is also transmitted by other Syrians:

- **[[Persons/Khālid ibn Ma'dān|Khālid ibn Maʿdān]]** ← Jubayr ibn Nufayr ← Abū Thaʿlaba (Musnad Aḥmad, Nasāʾī — [al-Musnad al-Jāmiʿ](https://shamela.ws/book/12748/8993))
- **Muslim ibn Mishkam** (kātib of Abū al-Dardāʾ, Damascene) ← Abū Thaʿlaba ([al-Musnad al-Jāmiʿ](https://shamela.ws/book/12748/8993))
- **[[Persons/Maymūn ibn Mahrān|Maymūn ibn Mahrān]]** ← Ibn ʿAbbās ([Musnad Aḥmad no. 2619](https://shamela.ws/book/25794/1887))

See the [[Traditions/Prohibition of carnivores|tradition page]].
