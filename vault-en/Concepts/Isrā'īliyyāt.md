# Isrā'īliyyāt

Traditions of **Judeo-Christian** origin (Torah, Talmud, rabbinic traditions, apocrypha) integrated into the Islamic tradition — Quranic exegesis, hadith, historiography.

## The "laundering" mechanism

```
Jewish/Christian tradition (Ka'b / Wahb)
    │
    └──→ Transmitted to stepson (Nawf) or to a Companion (Ibn 'Amr)
            │
            └──→ Reformulated as a "prophetic hadith"
                    │
                    └──→ Integrated into the canonical collections
```
